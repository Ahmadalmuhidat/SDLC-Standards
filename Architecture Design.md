## The methodologies adopted by the green touch technology
**Waterfall Development**
* Planning
* Analysis
* Design
* Development
* Deployment

**Phased Development**
breaks an overall system into a series of versions that are developed sequentially.

**Agile Development**
Deliver the system continuously through the development process.
The development process will be iterated through four phases:
* Planning 
* Analysis 
* Design 
* Development

## Criteria for Selecting a Methodology
* Clarity of user requirements.
* Familiarity with technology.
* System complexity.
* System reliability.
* Short time schedule.
* Schedule visibility.
